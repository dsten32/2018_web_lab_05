Join one of the fastest growing industries in the world

Technology is one of New Zealand’s fastest growing industries, and our third largest export earner. As a result there’s a huge demand for ICT graduates who can hit the ground running. That’s where the Auckland ICT Graduate School comes in. In a collaboration between the University of Auckland and the University of Waikato, we’ve created two postgraduate programmes to develop smart, industry-ready ICT graduates who will meet this need.

Career Pathways

The booming IT sector needs graduates with the ability to meet all of the opportunities on offer. The ICT Graduate School has been established to meet the need for well-qualified, industry-ready graduates, ready to step into ICT roles at a wide range of enterprises.

About Us

The Auckland ICT Graduate School is a joint initiative by the University of Auckland and the University of Waikato, established to meet the increasing demand for industry-ready ICT graduates. You’ll be taught at either our Auckland campus or our Hamilton campus, and at the premises of our industry partners. Your degree will be awarded by the university where you choose to study.

As well as connecting universities, we’re also forging a strong partnership between New Zealand’s tertiary and business sectors. We have long established links with some of New Zealand’s largest and most renowned companies.

Postgraduate Certificate in Information Technology


Kick-start your ICT career


Designed for students with a non ICT-related bachelors degree, our Postgraduate Certificate in Information Technology (PGCertInfoTech) will teach you the specialist skills you need to ignite your career in ICT.

It’s difficult to think of a business area that doesn’t rely on ICT, and ICT capability is integral to business success. ICT is the fastest growing industry in New Zealand, and businesses demand employees who have the skills to help drive this growth.

If you’ve ever considered working in the ICT sector, the PGCertInfoTech is the programme for you. The short, intensive programme is ideal for those who already have an undergraduate degree in a non ICT-related subject, and offers a supercharged route into the industry.

You’ll gain fundamental skills in software development, including up-to-date knowledge of object-oriented programming and design, web technologies and databases, which will complement your existing critical thinking and communication abilities.
